 Program that uses 64 bit mask to store info about students.
    Options:
    - Мark student attendance
    - Мark student absence
    - Show total missing students and their student numbers 
    - Show total attending students and their student numbers 
    - Change student status

Compile:
gcc studentsInfoMask.c funcs.c -o studentsInfoMask

Run:
./studentsInfoMask
